# Festival Concept Specification: Pixel & Frame Film Fest

## 1. Basic Festival Identity
* **Name:** Pixel and Frame Film Fest
* **Edition / Year:** 1st Edition, 2026
* **Location:** Prague, Czech Republic
* **Date Range:** September 10, 2026 - September 12, 2026

## 2. Target Audience & Event Character
* **Target Audience:** Cinema enthusiasts, animation students, aspiring filmmakers, and fans of independent art. The festival welcomes both industry professionals and the general public.
* **Event Character:** A 3-day celebration of visual storytelling, featuring film screenings, Q&A sessions with directors, and animation workshops. 
* **Genres/Themes:** Independent Cinema, 2D & 3D Animation, Short Films, Documentaries, and Experimental Video Art.

## 3. Website Structure
The festival website will feature a semantic, clean layout to highlight visual content:
* **Home (Introduction):** Landing page with the festival's manifesto, dates, location, and featured film posters.
* **Programme:** A 3-day schedule of screenings and panels, organized by time and cinema hall.
* **Performers:** A list of all 60 featured directors, animators, and guest speakers, with detail pages for their filmography.
* **Venues & Practical Information:** Details on the two main locations (Lumiere Main Cinema and Indie Projection Room), ticket info, and accommodation tips.
