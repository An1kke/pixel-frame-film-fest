Venues

Lumiere Main Cinema

Our primary location for feature films and major premieres.

Cinema Street 15, Prague 1

Capacity: 800 seats

Indie Projection Room

A more intimate space for workshops, short films, and Q&A sessions.

Art Alley 7, Prague 3

Capacity: 250 seats

Accommodation & Parking

  • Hotels: We recommend booking hotels in Prague 1 or Prague 3 for easy access to both venues.
  • Public Transport: Both venues are situated near major tram stops and subway lines.
  • Parking: Underground parking is available at the Lumiere Main Cinema. Spaces are limited.

Accessibility

Both venues are fully wheelchair accessible. Please contact us in advance if you require special seating arrangements.
